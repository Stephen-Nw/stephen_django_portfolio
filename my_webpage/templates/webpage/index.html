    About me

    Hello, my name is Stephen Nwaukwa. I am a self-taught Python developer, writer, and a CrossFit enthusiast. I enjoy developing programs and applications that serve a variety of purposes. Some applications I have developed are quirky and simply for my amusement while others have been to meet real needs for myself or people around me.

    My first foray into programming was right after I finished high school and was waiting for college. I grabbed a couple of books on Basic programming laying about the house and taught myself how to program. Using what I learned, I wrote several mathematical programs. In college, I further pursued my love of programming by taking courses in Fortran and writing more programs.

    After a detour of several years where I pursued a career in the healthcare industry, I returned to programming. Over a period of two years, I taught myself Python programming. Some of the modules that I have used to write some of my Python applications include Tkinter, Flask, Django, and Pandas, among others.

    I am a continuous learner, constantly exploring new technologies and languages. With technology constantly evolving, I believe it is important to stay on top on latest trends. Feel free to reach out to find out what I can do for you.
